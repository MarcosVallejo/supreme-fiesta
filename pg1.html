A simple countdown timer.

Example usage:

  • https://e.ggtimer.com/5minutes
  • https://e.ggtimer.com/1hour
  • https://e.ggtimer.com/30 (defaults to seconds)

Special Timers:

  • https://e.ggtimer.com/pomodoro (does one 25/5 minute cycle)
  • https://e.ggtimer.com/morning (can help get your blood pumping)
  • https://e.ggtimer.com/brushteeth (for healthy teeth)
  • https://e.ggtimer.com/tabata (8 reps of 20/10 second intervals)
